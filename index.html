AMATOR WE AWAIT YOUR SWEET RETURN

May 20, 2002 — In a stunning archaeological discovery, researchers have unearthed what appears to be the mythical Longevity Disc, a mysterious artifact; referenced only in obscure ancient texts, it was dismissed by most scholars as legend.

The disc, made of an unidentified alloy, contains an array of cryptic symbols and emits low-frequency vibrations that have yet to be explained. Early decoding efforts by international experts and skeptics have revealed only a single intelligible date:

June 11, 2025

What happens on this date is unknown. Theories range from global awakening to cataclysmic events. Governments have been tight-lipped. The full message of the disc remains hidden.

Who is Amator?

Ancient runes inscribed in the disc reference a being known as Amator, a court jester believed to have been gifted with divine insight. Serving as council to many kings, he provided both entertainment and insight; however not much is known about his life post-banishment. Some inscriptions found near the disc suggest a direct link between the artifact and this mysterious figure.

Legends say Amator "danced above life and eternity" and is considered the first true artist. It is theorized he may have encoded part of his prophecy within the Longevity Disc itself. Some researchers now believe Amator was no mere entertainer — but a conduit of cosmic knowledge.
